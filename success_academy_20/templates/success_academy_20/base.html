Welcome to SAY'2020

SAY - Success Academy for Youths is a flagship programme of the Deeper Life Schools Outreach (DLSO) for Youths that dates back as the very beginnings of the Deeper Christian Life Ministry. It's an annual programme that features seminars, workshops and soul-stiring exhortations from spirit-empowered men of GOD.

From the DLSO State Coordinator, Pastor Femi Ojelabi, I congratulate you for making it to this programme and do pray earnestly that GOD will meet you in this programme, Amen.
